Where it started?

Construction Logistics

During my tenure as a Logistics Manager in the construction sector in Brazil, I developed a keen expertise in managing complex supply chains and operational challenges specific to construction projects. This role required meticulous planning and coordination of materials, equipment, and labor to meet tight deadlines and budget constraints. I successfully implemented innovative logistic strategies that minimized waste and optimized resource allocation, significantly enhancing project efficiency. My hands-on experience in navigating the unique regulatory and environmental challenges in Brazil further enriched my skill set, allowing me to deliver projects effectively while adhering to compliance standards. This role not only sharpened my logistical acumen but also deepened my understanding of the critical interplay between on-site operations and overall project success.

Supply Chain Optimization

Streamlined supply chain processes to enhance material delivery efficiency and reduce construction delays.

Regulatory Compliance

Navigated complex regulatory environments to ensure all logistics operations adhered to local and national standards.

Project Management

Led cross-functional teams to successful project completions, ensuring on-time delivery within budget constraints.

MBA Insights

My journey through the MBA program, with a focus on Operations and Supply Chain, provided me with profound insights and a deeper understanding of the strategic and analytical aspects of management. This experience sharpened my ability to tackle complex challenges in supply chain logistics and operations, through a curriculum that blended theoretical knowledge with practical applications. Courses like Lean Management and Global Supply Chain Strategies not only broadened my perspective but also equipped me with the tools to drive efficiency and innovation. Engaging with peers from diverse backgrounds fostered a collaborative learning environment, while case studies and projects linked to real-world scenarios honed my decision-making skills. This MBA has been instrumental in my professional growth, enabling me to implement effective operational strategies and lead teams with confidence.

Amazon Internship

Miami - FL

  • 2023

    Internship

    During my internship at Amazon in Miami, I had the opportunity to work at one of the bustling delivery stations, a pivotal role that immersed me in the heart of Amazon's complex logistics and distribution operations. This experience provided me with a firsthand look at the operational challenges and efficiencies within a major fulfillment network. My responsibilities ranged from overseeing the accurate sorting and dispatching of packages to optimizing route planning and delivery schedules. This position not only honed my skills in logistics management but also enhanced my ability to work effectively under pressure, ensuring that all deliveries met Amazon's stringent timeframes and quality standards. The internship was instrumental in developing my practical knowledge of supply chain dynamics, particularly in a high-volume, fast-paced environment, preparing me for more significant responsibilities in the field.

Full Time Job

Gabriel Zacheu

Operations Manager

In June, I will embark on an exciting new chapter with Amazon in Las Vegas, focusing on Same Day Delivery operations. This role represents a significant step forward in my career, emphasizing my capacity for leadership within high-stakes operational environments. As a leader, my goal will be to drive the efficiency and effectiveness of same-day delivery services, a critical component of Amazon's commitment to customer satisfaction. I plan to leverage my extensive background in operations and logistics management to optimize delivery processes, enhance team performance, and ensure that we exceed service delivery standards. This opportunity not only aligns with my expertise in fast-paced operational settings but also challenges me to innovate and improve upon existing systems to achieve remarkable results in real-time logistics.
